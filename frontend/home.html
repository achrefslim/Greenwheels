"Tunisia's Pollution Crisis: A Growing Threat to Health and Environment"...

Pollution in Tunisia is a rising concern, with urban areas like Tunis experiencing air pollution levels that exceed World Health Organization's safe limits. In 2020, PM2.5 levels in Tunis were over 30% higher than recommended. Water bodies and beaches also face pollution, worsened by inadequate waste management. This environmental degradation is linked to an increase in respiratory diseases, highlighting the urgent need for action to address the crisis.

"Green Wheels: Revolutionizing Street Cleanliness with Smart Garbage Collection"

Green Wheels is an innovative robot designed to collect garbage from the streets, ensuring cleaner environments and promoting sustainability. The robot features a sophisticated system with a container to store collected waste, an arm to pick up garbage, and a conveyor belt to transport it to separate compartments for recycling. Equipped with advanced sensors and cameras, Green Wheels can identify and sort different types of waste, such as plastic, paper, and organic materials, directing them to the appropriate bins. With its sleek, truck-like design and efficient functionality, Green Wheels is a powerful solution for waste management, making urban spaces cleaner and greener.

Green Wheels platform: Effortless Control for a Cleaner Future

The Green Wheels website is a user-friendly platform designed to simplify the control and monitoring of the Green Wheels robot. It allows users to easily operate the robot, track its progress, and manage its features with just a few clicks. Packed with innovative tools, the website offers real-time updates, detailed analytics, and customizable settings to optimize garbage collection efficiency. Discover a range of advanced functionalities that make managing Green Wheels effortless, ensuring cleaner streets and a greener future.
